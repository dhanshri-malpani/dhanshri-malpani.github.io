About the Artist

Artist Profile

Dhanshri Kabra Malpani creates mandala-inspired and fine-line artworks that blend structure, calmness, and visual identity. She is a 2x Asia Book of Record holder in typography, and her portfolio reflects discipline, detail, and artistic confidence.

Her work is ideal for students who want to learn focus, symmetry, hand control, and creative flow. Every composition is designed to feel premium, expressive, and deeply personal.

This site is intentionally built as a clean, mobile-first art brand experience so that the work feels modern while still preserving the handmade spirit of the drawings.
